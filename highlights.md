# Highlights

Mapping the landscape of supply chain management research: a comprehensive bibliometric analysis of 10,000 Scopus publications (2025–2026)

- 10,000 Scopus supply chain papers (2025–2026) analysed bibliometrically.
- Output grew ~63% year-on-year; China and India produced 46% of papers.
- AI and machine learning appear in over 11% of all author keywords.
- LDA modelling uncovers eight coherent research clusters from abstracts.
- Sustainability acts as the field's central organising principle.
